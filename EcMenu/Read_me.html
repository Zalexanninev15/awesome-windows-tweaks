EC Menu

 

1. Add a Ec Menu entry to your right click

2. Delete Ec Menu entry from your right click menu

3. Enable shift click

4. Move a sub menu entry from submenu  to the desktop context menu (How to enable shift click for a sub menu item)

5. Add a Program to right click menu

6. How to remove  unnecessary entries that you no longer need (How to clean your context menu)

7. How can we Unblock a blocked .txt. folder , program  etc.
